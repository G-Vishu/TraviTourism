Paris

I just loved my visit to the Eiffel Tower. This was my first visit and I was amazed at how big it is in person. It stands much wider than it looks on the pictures I have seen. I heard the stories about the pick-pockets around the Eiffel tower so I was extra careful but I didn't see any incidents with the group I was with. The second level is a great place to enjoy the view and see the whole of Paris! The elevators were a bit crowded so I decided to take the stairs down to the 1st level.
                       ---by Eliza Joseph
